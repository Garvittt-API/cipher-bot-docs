## 🔒 Privacy Policy

<details>
<summary><b>Click to expand Privacy Policy</b></summary>
<br>

**Effective Date:** June 21, 2026

Your privacy matters. This section details exactly what data Cipher handles, why we need it, and how we protect it.

### 1. Data We Collect & Store
As an all-in-one multipurpose bot, Cipher only processes data strictly necessary for its features:
*   **User Data:** Discord User IDs, usernames, and avatars (to track economy/XP progression and identify users in moderation logs).
*   **Server Data:** Guild IDs, Channel IDs, and Role IDs (to remember your custom configurations, prefix changes, logging channels, and auto-mod setups).
*   **Message Content:** Cipher **does not log or read regular chat history**. It only dynamically processes message content when explicitly triggered by a command or an active auto-moderation filter.

### 2. How We Use & Share Data
*   We use your data strictly to execute commands (e.g., executing a mute, displaying a level card, or firing a welcome message).
*   **Zero Third-Party Sharing:** We never sell, rent, trade, or share your server or user data with anyone.

### 3. Data Deletion & Retention
*   **Server Removal:** If Cipher is kicked from a server, its configuration data is safely deleted from our database `[immediately / within 30 days]`.
*   **Manual Request:** You can request a complete wipe of your user or server data at any time by joining our support server.
